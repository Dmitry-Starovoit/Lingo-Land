    How to learn English with a movie?

    You need to learn English with those films that you like. Because when watching your favorite movie you do not get tired , and it allows for in-depth language learning. While watching the movie you will learn a lot of phrases , idioms and interesting words that will come in handy. Understanding English by ear is one of the main stages of learning a language. You need to get used to perceiving by ear, since familiar words do not always sound from the carrier , as expected. With your favorite movie, you will significantly increase your level of knowledge. Understanding English is now very important. If you are just a beginner in learning English language, you can watch with subtitles , but do not abuse the subtitles. After watching a piece of the film , write out the words you do not know and study them, how and where they are used, with what intonation and stress.

    Watch this same part of the movie over and over again until you fully understand it.

    If it is difficult to perceive by ear and it seems to you that the carrier speaks too quickly, then reduce the playback speed
